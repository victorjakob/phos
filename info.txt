Design Vibe:
Create a full-screen, modern, celestial-inspired landing page for PHOS, a global peace movement happening August 12, 2026.
The style should be elegant, minimal, and immersive, with smooth fade/slide animations, large breathing space, and a color palette of deep midnight blue, eclipse gold, moon white, and starlight gray.
Use Next.js 14 App Router + Tailwind CSS, mobile-first responsive design, and clean typography.
Include subtle parallax background stars and a gentle logo animation of 3 circles aligning in golden ratio.
Add language toggle (EN/IS) in top right, switching between content from two text files.
Buttons and CTAs should be rounded, glowing softly on hover, with gold-to-white gradients.
Overall mood: sacred, cosmic, unifying — like an invitation to a global ceremony.
